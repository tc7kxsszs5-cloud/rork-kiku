# Финансовая модель Rork-Kiku: Обзор

## Три сценария развития

Финансовая модель построена на трёх сценариях, отражающих различные траектории роста компании в зависимости от market adoption, execution quality и external factors.

---

## Сценарий 1: Консервативный

### Предположения

**User Growth:**
- Медленный organic growth
- Limited marketing budget
- Conservative conversion rates
- Year 1: 3K users (300 paid)
- Year 5: 1M users (100K paid)

**Pricing:**
- $99/year premium subscription
- Free → Paid conversion: 10%
- Churn rate: 30% (Year 1) → 25% (Year 5)

**Market Penetration:**
- Фокус только на России в первые 2 года
- Slow international expansion
- 0.5% penetration к Year 5

### Финансовые показатели

| Year | Users (Total) | Paid Users | ARR | EBITDA | Team Size |
|------|---------------|------------|-----|--------|-----------|
| 2026 | 3,000 | 300 | $30K | -$600K | 5 |
| 2027 | 20,000 | 2,000 | $200K | -$800K | 8 |
| 2028 | 100,000 | 10,000 | $1M | -$2M | 15 |
| 2029 | 400,000 | 40,000 | $4M | -$1M | 25 |
| 2030 | 1,000,000 | 100,000 | $10M | $500K | 35 |

### Cost Structure (Year 3)

- **Team**: 55% ($1.1M) — 15 people, conservative salaries
- **Infrastructure**: 15% ($300K) — AWS, ML costs
- **Marketing**: 20% ($400K) — limited paid acquisition
- **R&D**: 5% ($100K)
- **Other**: 5% ($100K)
- **Total**: $2M

### Funding Need

- **Seed**: $1M (18-24 месяца runway)
- **Series A**: $5M (Year 3)
- **Total raised**: $6M
- **Dilution**: ~40% by Year 5

### Key Risks in This Scenario

- Slow growth может не привлечь top talent
- Limited marketing → low brand awareness
- Может не достичь escape velocity для Series A
- Profitability отложена на Year 5+

## Допущения и методология

### User Acquisition

**Channels & CAC:**
- **Organic** (word-of-mouth, PR): $0-10 CAC
- **Partnerships** (schools, telecoms): $20-30 CAC
- **Paid marketing** (social ads): $40-60 CAC
- **Blended CAC**: $30-50 (Base case)

**Conversion funnel:**
1. Awareness → App download: 10-20%
2. Download → Registration: 60-80%
3. Registration → Active use: 70-90%
4. Free → Paid: 10-25% (зависит от сценария)

### Pricing Strategy

**Premium Subscription:**
- **Monthly**: $9.99/month ($120/year)
- **Annual**: $99/year (17% discount для annual)
- **Assumption**: 70% выбирают annual plan

**Enterprise/School:**
- **Small** (50-100 students): $499/month
- **Medium** (100-500 students): $999/month
- **Large** (500+ students): Custom pricing

### Retention & Churn

**Cohort retention:**
- M1: 90% (onboarding drop-off)
- M3: 75%
- M6: 65%
- M12: 60%
- M24: 50%
- M36: 45%

**Annual churn:**
- Year 1: 25-30% (high early churn)
- Year 2: 22-25%
- Year 3+: 15-20% (stable)

### Cost Assumptions

**Team costs:**
- **Engineer** (Russia): $40-80K/year
- **Engineer** (US/EU): $120-180K/year
- **Non-tech** (Russia): $30-50K/year
- **Non-tech** (US/EU): $60-100K/year
- **Fully-loaded** (salary + benefits + overhead): 1.4x

**Infrastructure:**
- **AWS compute**: $0.02-0.05 per user/month
- **ML inference** (GPU): $1-3 per 1000 inferences
- **Storage** (S3): $0.023 per GB/month
- **CDN** (CloudFront): $0.085 per GB transfer
- **Database** (RDS): $500-5000/month (depends on scale)

**Marketing:**
- **Performance ads**: $30-60 CPA
- **PR & content**: $5K-20K/month
- **Events & partnerships**: $10K-50K/quarter

### Geographic Expansion

**Russia:**
- Total addressable: 15M families
- Penetration: 0.5-2% (Year 5)
- ARPU: $80-100/year (локализованная цена)

**US:**
- Total addressable: 30M families
- Penetration: 0.5-1.5% (Year 5)
- ARPU: $120-140/year (higher willingness to pay)

**EU:**
- Total addressable: 25M families
- Penetration: 0.3-1% (Year 5)
- ARPU: $100-120/year

**Asia:**
- Total addressable: 150M families
- Penetration: 0.1-0.5% (Year 5)
- ARPU: $50-80/year (price-sensitive market)

## Sensitivity Analysis

### Key Variables Impacting Outcomes

**User Growth Rate:**
- +/- 20% в росте пользователей → +/- 30-40% в revenue (Year 3-5)
- Network effects amplify growth или decline

**Conversion Rate:**
- +5% в conversion (15% вместо 10%) → +50% revenue
- Критически важен для unit economics

**Churn Rate:**
- +5% в churn (25% вместо 20%) → -20-30% LTV
- Retention = key для долгосрочного success

**CAC:**
- CAC $50 вместо $30 → требуется больше funding, позже break-even
- Organic channels критичны для снижения CAC

**Pricing:**
- $120/year вместо $99 → +20% revenue (если не impact conversion)
- Need to test willingness to pay

## Рекомендации

- Focus на profitability раньше
- Bootstrap подход, minimal funding

- Balanced growth & sustainability
- Raise Seed + Series A

- Aim для break-even к Year 4-5

- Aggressive growth at all costs
- Raise multiple rounds
- Rapid geographic expansion
- Aim для unicorn trajectory
