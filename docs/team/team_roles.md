Этот документ описывает ключевые роли в команде Rork-Kiku и их зоны ответственности. Команда строится по мере роста компании.

- **Team**: 2 co-founders (CEO/CTO)
- **Focus**: Product development, pilot
- **Timeline**: Months 0-6

### Seed Round ($1-2M)
- **Add**: 
  - 1-2 Backend Engineers
  - 1 iOS Developer
  - 1 ML Engineer (contract или full-time)

## Compensation Philosophy

**Principles:**
- Competitive salaries для market
- Equity для early employees (meaningful ownership)
- Benefits: health insurance, remote work flexibility
- Performance bonuses tied к company success

**Equity Guidelines (early stage):**
- Co-founders: 40-50% each (fully diluted)
- First engineer: 0.5-1.5%
- Early employees (< 10): 0.2-0.8%
- Later employees: 0.05-0.3%
- Advisors: 0.1-0.5%

**Vesting:**
- Standard 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff
- Accelerated vesting при acquisition (single или double trigger)
