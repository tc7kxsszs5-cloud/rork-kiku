# Роли и обязанности команды Rork-Kiku

## Обзор

Данный документ описывает ключевые роли в команде Rork-Kiku, их обязанности и навыки.

**Размер команды:**
- MVP (Pre-Seed): 5-7 человек
- Pilot (Seed): 10-15 человек
- Production (Series A): 20-30 человек
- Scale (Series B): 50+ человек

## 9. Compensation Guidelines (Ориентировочно, для России/Remote)

### Founders:
- Pre-Seed: minimal salary ($30-50K/year) + equity (50-70% split между founders)
- Seed: reasonable salary ($80-100K/year) + equity

### Engineers (Mid-level):
- Salary: $60-90K/year (зависит от location, remote)
- Equity: 0.5-1.5% (early employees)

### Engineers (Senior):
- Salary: $90-120K/year
- Equity: 1-2%

### Product/Design:
- Salary: $70-100K/year
- Equity: 0.5-1.5%

### Moderators:
- Hourly: $15-20/hour (contractor)
- Или: $30-40K/year (full-time)

### Managers (Growth, Sales):
- Salary: $70-100K/year
- Equity: 0.5-1%

**Note:** Compensation зависит от:
- Location (США выше, Россия/Восточная Европа ниже)
- Stage (early = lower salary, higher equity)
- Experience level
- Market conditions

**Equity vesting:**
- 4 years vesting
- 1 year cliff
- Monthly vesting после cliff

## 10. Team Culture и Values

### Core Values:
1. **Child Safety First** — безопасность детей превыше всего
2. **Transparency** — открытость в коммуникации
3. **Ownership** — каждый owner своей области
4. **Continuous Learning** — постоянное развитие
5. **Collaboration** — teamwork и взаимопомощь

### Work Environment:
- **Remote-first** (distributed team)
- **Flexible hours** (в разумных пределах, overlap для встреч)
- **Async communication** (документация, Slack)
- **Regular meetings:** daily standups, weekly all-hands

### Benefits (Post-Seed):
- Health insurance
- Paid time off (20+ days/year)
- Parental leave
- Learning budget ($1,000/year)
- Co-working allowance (remote)
- Equipment budget (laptop, monitor, etc.)
