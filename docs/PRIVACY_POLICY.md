# Privacy Policy - KIKU Child Safety Platform

**Effective Date:** January 2, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to KIKU, a child safety monitoring platform designed to protect children from cyberbullying, harassment, inappropriate content, and other online dangers. We take the privacy and safety of children very seriously.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our service.

## Who We Are

**Service Name:** KIKU  
**Service Type:** Child Safety Monitoring Platform  
**Contact:** privacy@kiku-app.com  
**Data Protection Officer:** dpo@kiku-app.com

## Age Restrictions

### Children Under 18
- KIKU is designed exclusively for children and teenagers under 18 years old
- Parental consent is required for all users under 18
- Parents must create an account before children can use the service

### Adults
- Only parents and legal guardians of registered children may create accounts
- Adult accounts are used solely for monitoring and protecting children

## Information We Collect

### For Child Accounts (with parental consent):

**Personal Information:**
- First name only (last name optional)
- Age or date of birth
- Device identifier (for session management)

**Usage Information:**
- Chat messages (for safety monitoring)
- Voice messages (transcribed for analysis)
- Images shared (analyzed for safety)
- App usage patterns
- Alert history

**Location Information:**
- Location data is collected ONLY during SOS emergency situations
- Location sharing can be disabled by parents except for SOS

### For Parent Accounts:

**Personal Information:**
- Full name
- Email address or phone number
- Verification status

**Account Information:**
- Login credentials (encrypted)
- Linked child accounts
- Parental control settings
- Compliance and consent records

## How We Use Information

### Primary Purpose: Child Safety

We use collected information exclusively to:

1. **Monitor for Safety Threats**
   - Detect cyberbullying and harassment
   - Identify inappropriate content
   - Flag potential predators
   - Analyze risk levels in conversations

2. **Provide Safety Features**
   - SOS emergency alerts
   - Real-time threat notifications
   - Safety recommendations
   - Age-appropriate content filtering

3. **Enable Parental Controls**
   - Activity monitoring
   - Contact management
   - Screen time limits
   - Safety reports

4. **Comply with Legal Obligations**
   - Maintain audit trails
   - Respond to legal requests
   - Protect children's welfare

### What We DON'T Do:

- ❌ Sell or share data with third parties
- ❌ Use data for advertising or marketing
- ❌ Create behavioral profiles for commercial purposes
- ❌ Share data with schools or employers
- ❌ Use data for AI training (without explicit consent)

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Data is Stored

- **Primary Storage:** Local device storage (AsyncStorage)
- **Optional Cloud Backup:** Only with explicit parent consent
- **Server Processing:** Minimal - only for AI analysis when necessary

### Security Measures

1. **Encryption**
   - End-to-end encryption for messages
   - Encrypted storage on device
   - Secure data transmission (TLS 1.3+)

2. **Access Controls**
   - Strong authentication required
   - Parent-only access to settings
   - Role-based permissions

3. **Regular Audits**
   - Security assessments
   - Vulnerability scanning
   - Penetration testing

4. **Data Minimization**
   - Collect only necessary data
   - Automatic deletion of old data
   - No unnecessary backups

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Deletion Process |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------|
| Active messages | 90 days | Automatic |
| Resolved alerts | 1 year | Automatic |
| User accounts | Until deleted | Manual by parent |
| Compliance logs | 7 years | Automatic |
| SOS records | 2 years | Automatic |

### Data Deletion

Parents can delete:
- Individual messages or conversations
- Alert history
- Child's entire account
- Their own parent account

Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

## Parental Rights and Controls

### As a Parent, You Have the Right To:

1. **Access** - View all data collected about your child
2. **Correct** - Update or correct inaccurate information
3. **Delete** - Remove your child's account and all data
4. **Export** - Download all data in machine-readable format
5. **Object** - Opt-out of specific data collection
6. **Restrict** - Limit how data is processed

### How to Exercise Your Rights:

- **In-App:** Profile → Settings → Privacy Controls
- **Email:** privacy@kiku-app.com
- **Response Time:** Within 30 days

## Children's Privacy Rights

### Under COPPA (United States)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act:
- Verifiable parental consent before data collection
- Clear notice of data practices
- Limited data collection
- No conditioning service on excess data collection
- Parents can review and delete child's data

### Under GDPR (European Union)

We comply with GDPR Article 8 (conditions for child's consent):
- Parental consent for children under 16 (or lower, depending on member state)
- Reasonable efforts to verify parental consent
- Enhanced protections for children's data
- Clear, plain language privacy notices

### Under CCPA (California)

We provide California consumers:
- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of data sales (N/A - we don't sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination

## International Data Transfers

**Current Status:** All data stored locally on device
- No international data transfers by default
- Optional cloud backup with explicit consent
- Data residency respects parent's location

**If We Add Cloud Storage:**
- Comply with Privacy Shield/Standard Contractual Clauses
- Store data in parent's region when possible

## AI and Automated Decision-Making

### How We Use AI

**Content Analysis:**
- Detect harmful content in text and images
- Assess risk levels in conversations
- Generate safety recommendations

**Important Limitations:**
- AI is not perfect and may have false positives/negatives
- Parents retain full control over all decisions
- No fully automated actions affecting child safety

**Transparency:**
- Parents can see AI analysis results
- Explanations provided for high-risk flags
- Human review available on request

### AI Data Usage

- AI analysis data is not stored permanently
- Results used only for immediate safety assessment
- No AI training on child data without consent
- Anonymous threat patterns may be analyzed for service improvement
