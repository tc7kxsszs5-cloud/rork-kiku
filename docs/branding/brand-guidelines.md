## Imagery Style

### Photos

**Style:**
- Natural, candid moments
- Family-focused (parents + children)
- Diverse families (—Ä–∞–∑–Ω—ã–µ ethnicities, family structures)
- Bright, warm lighting
- Genuine emotions (not stock photo smiles)

**Avoid:**
- Overly staged photos
- Single child alone (emphasize family)
- Dark or moody imagery
- Children's faces too prominent (privacy)

### Illustrations

**Style:**
- Friendly, approachable
- Simple, clean lines
- Soft colors (brand palette)
- Relatable characters

**Usage:**
- Onboarding screens
- Empty states
- Error messages
- Help/tutorial sections

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast

**Minimum ratios (WCAG AA):**
- Normal text: 4.5:1
- Large text (18px+): 3:1
- UI elements: 3:1

**Tools:**
- [Contrast Checker](https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/)
- Lighthouse (Chrome DevTools)

### Alt Text

**For images:**
- Descriptive alt text (not "image")
- Explain context
- Keep concise (< 125 characters)

### Typography

**Readable text:**
- Min font size: 16px (mobile)
- Line height: 1.5
- Avoid all caps (hard to read)
- Left-align text (not justified)
