## Assumptions & Risks

### Assumptions

- **Product-market fit validated** в Q2 2026 pilot
- **Funding secured** ($1.5M Seed в Q3 2026, $5-10M Series A в Q2 2027)
- **Team scaling** успешен (hiring 20-30 people by end 2027)
- **Competition** не запускает аналог (или мы maintain competitive edge)

### Risks

**Risk 1: Pilot не проходит валидацию**
- Mitigation: pivot или adjust value proposition

**Risk 2: Fundraising занимает дольше**
- Mitigation: extend runway через cost optimization

**Risk 3: Regulatory changes (COPPA/GDPR)**
- Mitigation: proactive compliance, legal counsel

**Risk 4: Competition enters**
- Mitigation: first-mover advantage, strong brand, superior UX
