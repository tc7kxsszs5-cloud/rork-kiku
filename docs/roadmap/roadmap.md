## Risk Mitigation по фазам

### 2026 Q1-Q2 Risks:
- **Risk:** Technical complexity too high
- **Mitigation:** Simplify MVP, focus на core features only

- **Risk:** Low pilot recruitment
- **Mitigation:** Early partnership outreach, multiple channels

### 2026 Q3-Q4 Risks:
- **Risk:** App Store rejection
- **Mitigation:** Early compliance review, over-compliance с guidelines

- **Risk:** Low public adoption
- **Mitigation:** Strong pilot testimonials, partnerships, free tier

### 2027 Q1-Q2 Risks:
- **Risk:** Fundraise fails
- **Mitigation:** Strong metrics pre-raise, multiple investors в pipeline

- **Risk:** Scaling infrastructure costs
- **Mitigation:** Cost monitoring, optimization, reserve buffer

### 2027 Q3-Q4 Risks:
- **Risk:** International compliance complexity
- **Mitigation:** Local legal counsel, phased rollout

- **Risk:** Competition increases
- **Mitigation:** First-mover advantage, brand building, partnerships
