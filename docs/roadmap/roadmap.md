## Risks & Mitigation

### Product Risks:
- **Low adoption:** Mitigation: pilot feedback, partnerships, marketing
- **Poor retention:** Mitigation: continuous product improvement, user research
- **Moderation failures:** Mitigation: human oversight, ML improvement, escalation

### Business Risks:
- **Slow fundraising:** Mitigation: extend runway, revenue focus
- **Competition:** Mitigation: differentiation, fast execution, partnerships
- **Regulatory changes:** Mitigation: legal counsel, proactive compliance

### Technical Risks:
- **Scalability issues:** Mitigation: load testing, auto-scaling, monitoring
- **Security breaches:** Mitigation: security audits, penetration testing, incident response
- **Third-party dependencies:** Mitigation: vendor diversification, fallback plans
