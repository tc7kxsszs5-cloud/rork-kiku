## Risks and Mitigations

### Market Risks

1. **Competition from Big Tech** (Apple, Google improve parental controls)
   - **Mitigation**: Superior features, partnerships, niche focus (child safety experts)

2. **Regulatory changes** (stricter COPPA, GDPR)
   - **Mitigation**: Proactive compliance, legal advisors, industry participation

3. **Market saturation** (too many competitors)
   - **Mitigation**: Differentiation via AI, UX, focus on outcomes (not just blocking)

### Product Risks

1. **False positives** (blocking safe content)
   - **Mitigation**: Continuous ML model improvement, feedback loop, human review

2. **Kids bypassing controls**
   - **Mitigation**: Multi-layer protection, education, parent engagement

3. **Privacy concerns** (parents worried about surveillance)
   - **Mitigation**: Transparency, clear policies, privacy-first design

### Operational Risks

1. **Scaling challenges** (infrastructure, team)
   - **Mitigation**: Experienced SRE, cloud-native architecture, hiring plan

2. **Fundraising delays**
   - **Mitigation**: Extend runway, cost controls, revenue focus

3. **Key person risk** (founders)
   - **Mitigation**: Strong leadership team, succession planning, documentation
