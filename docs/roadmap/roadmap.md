## Risk Mitigation по фазам

### Q1-Q2 2026 (MVP Development)
**Risks:**
- Technical delays, team not assembled
- ML model не достаточно accurate

**Mitigation:**
- Clear MVP scope (minimal features)
- Hire experienced engineers
- Use pre-trained models (fine-tune)

### Q3 2026 (Pilot)
**Risks:**
- Can't recruit 50-100 families
- Poor user feedback (low NPS)

**Mitigation:**
- Multiple partnership channels
- Incentives (free Premium)
- Quick iteration на feedback

### Q4 2026 - Q1 2027 (Public Launch)
**Risks:**
- User acquisition expensive (high CAC)
- Low conversion (free → paid)

**Mitigation:**
- Freemium model (lower barrier)
- Organic channels (partnerships, PR)
- Clear value proposition

### Q2-Q4 2027 (Scale & Global)
**Risks:**
- Competition (big players копируют)
- Regulatory hurdles (COPPA, GDPR)
- Operational scale (moderation)

**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage, network effects
- Compliance-first approach, legal team
- Invest в ML, модерация scalability
