# KIKU Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 2, 2026  
**Last Updated**: January 2, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to KIKU ("we," "our," or "us"). KIKU is a child safety monitoring application designed to help parents and guardians protect their children in digital communications. We are committed to protecting the privacy and safety of all our users, especially children.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the KIKU mobile application ("App"). We take child privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other applicable privacy laws.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

**For All Users:**
- Name
- Role (Parent or Child)
- Date of Birth (for age verification)
- Email address (optional, for parents)
- Device identifier
- Language preference

**For Parent Accounts:**
- Email addresses for guardian notifications
- Phone numbers for emergency contacts (optional)

**For Child Accounts:**
- Parent/guardian account linkage
- Age-verified information
- Content filter level (automatically set based on age)

### 2. Monitoring Data

**To provide safety monitoring services, we collect:**
- Text messages being monitored
- Chat participants and metadata
- AI analysis results (risk assessments)
- Safety alerts and notifications
- Images shared in monitored conversations (with appropriate filtering)

### 3. Safety & Emergency Information

- SOS alert data (including location when activated)
- Whitelisted and blocked contacts
- Time restriction settings
- Usage statistics (screen time, message counts)

### 4. Compliance & Audit Data

- Parental consent records
- Settings change logs
- Data access requests
- Account modification history

### 5. Technical Information

- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Error logs and crash reports (anonymized)
- Performance metrics (anonymized)

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Purpose: Child Safety

We use collected information solely for:

1. **Safety Monitoring**: Analyzing messages and content for potential risks
2. **Parental Notifications**: Alerting guardians to safety concerns
3. **Emergency Response**: Facilitating SOS alerts with location data
4. **Content Filtering**: Applying age-appropriate content filters
5. **Access Control**: Managing whitelisted contacts and time restrictions

### Secondary Purposes

- **App Improvement**: Anonymized data to improve safety features
- **Legal Compliance**: Maintaining records as required by law
- **Security**: Protecting against misuse and fraud
- **Support**: Responding to user inquiries and issues

### What We Do NOT Do

- ❌ We do NOT sell or rent personal information
- ❌ We do NOT use data for advertising or marketing
- ❌ We do NOT share data with third parties for their purposes
- ❌ We do NOT track users across other apps or websites
- ❌ We do NOT create user profiles for marketing

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Architecture

**KIKU uses a "local-first" approach:**
- All user data is stored locally on your device
- No data is uploaded to cloud servers except when explicitly required for features you enable
- You maintain full control over your data
- Works fully offline

### Storage Locations

All data is stored in your device's secure storage using React Native AsyncStorage:
- User profiles and settings
- Monitored messages and analysis results
- Safety alerts and SOS records
- Compliance logs

### Security Measures

- ✅ **Device-level encryption**: iOS Keychain and Android Keystore
- ✅ **Secure communication**: All network requests use HTTPS/TLS
- ✅ **No plaintext passwords**: Authentication uses secure methods
- ✅ **Regular security updates**: Prompt updates for security patches
- ✅ **Access controls**: Parent authentication for sensitive settings

### Data Retention

- **Active accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Compliance logs**: Retained for 3 years as required by law
- **Backups**: No automatic cloud backups; users control backups via device

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

### Special Protections for Children Under 13

KIKU complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

#### Verifiable Parental Consent
- Before collecting any personal information from a child under 13, we require verifiable parental consent
- Parents must create a parent account and link their child's account
- Consent is documented with timestamp and version tracking

#### Parental Rights
Parents of children under 13 have the right to:
- ✅ Review all information collected about their child
- ✅ Refuse to permit further collection of information
- ✅ Delete their child's account and all associated data
- ✅ Modify or correct their child's information

#### Limited Data Collection
For children under 13, we collect only:
- Name and age (for age verification)
- Monitored messages (for safety purposes)
- SOS alerts (for emergency response)
- No email, no phone number, no other personal identifiers

#### No Third-Party Sharing
- We do NOT share children's information with third parties
- No advertising or marketing to children
- No behavioral tracking or profiling

### How to Exercise Parental Rights

To review, modify, or delete your child's information:
1. Log into your parent account
2. Navigate to Profile settings
3. Select your child's linked account
4. Choose the action you wish to take

Or contact us at: privacy@kiku-app.com

## Age Verification and Content Filtering

### Age Verification Process
1. Users provide their date of birth during account creation
2. Age is calculated and verified
3. Appropriate content filter level is automatically assigned

### Content Filter Levels

**Strict (Under 13):**
- Maximum AI moderation sensitivity
- All unknown contacts blocked
- Parental approval required for all contacts
- Comprehensive content filtering

**Moderate (Ages 13-15):**
- Enhanced AI moderation
- Restricted contact approval
- Age-appropriate content filtering

**Minimal (Ages 16+):**
- Standard AI moderation
- User-controlled settings with parental oversight

Parents can always override automatic settings for stricter controls.
